WINE MAKING

Trutta is inspired by the pioneering spirit of our ancestors who left Cornwall for the gold rush hills of Gippsland in the late 1800s.

The Mountains of West Gippsland have been a place of reflection, contemplation, and inspiration for four generations of Trutta fishermen.

These mountains represent an honest pioneering spirit.

Trutta Wines are handcrafted in Central Victoria to produce honest wines, with a strong sense of place.

Making wine with no additions, where possible, to create drinkable wines with integrity.

2017 ROSÉ

Predominantly Shiraz from our Harcourt North vineyard, with a touch of Mourvedre from Heathcote. Organic practices in the vineyard for over 20 years. Oxidative juice handling, and basket pressed to build body and structure. Aged 6 months on lees, with occasional stirring. Full malolactic fermentation. Indigenous yeasts, and only a touch of SO2 at bottling.

2017 MARSANNE

From our Harcourt Valley vineyard. Organic practices in the vineyard for 6 years. Oxidative juice handling, and basket pressed to build body and structure. Aged 6 months in a combination of stainless and old oak, occasional lees stirring. Full malolactic fermentation. Indigenous yeasts, and only a touch of SO2 at bottling.
